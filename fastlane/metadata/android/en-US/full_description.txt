HabitFlow is a 100% FREE, privacy-first habit and task tracker built for those who value their data.

🎯 CORE FEATURES
• Create, track, and manage daily habits & tasks
• Support for daily, weekly, monthly, and one-time schedules
• Build streaks and celebrate milestones (7, 30, 100+ day streaks)
• Rich task notes and custom categories
• Voice input for quick habit logging

📊 ANALYTICS & INSIGHTS
• Beautiful completion charts and progress visualization
• Weekly and monthly reports
• Calendar heatmap view (GitHub style)
• Current and longest streak tracking
• Completion percentage for each habit

🎮 GAMIFICATION
• XP points system for completions
• Achievement badges and milestones
• Daily challenges to stay motivated
• Level progression with visual rewards

🔔 SMART REMINDERS
• Local notifications for habit reminders
• Custom reminder times for each habit

• No cloud dependency - reminders work offline

💾 DATA MANAGEMENT

• Complete data portability
